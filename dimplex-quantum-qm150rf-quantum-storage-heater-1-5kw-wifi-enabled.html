Dimplex Quantum QM150 HHR 1.5kW storage heater. One of the most advanced modern slimline high retention night storage heaters on the market, highly advanced controls provide superior efficiency over all other brands. By monitoring the weather as well as your energy usage this electric heater provides an ultra efficient tailored heating solution for any environment, home or work place.

Due to a critical component shortage, Dimplex have temporarily stopped production of the smart version of this heater - Dimplex Quantum QM150RF. This storage heater, the Dimplex QM150 is the exact same 1.5kW storage heater manufactured without the onboard wifi module to allow additional control via the Dimplex Smartphone App.

Why are Dimplex Quantum's better than all the other storage heaters?

Its all in the iQ controller, superior intelligent automatic charge control calculations drive its efficiency to another level. The clever algorithm takes into account your average usage, lifestyle, and even the external climate conditions, to work out the optimal amount of energy needed to reach your temperature at your desired time, ensuring no money or energy is wasted. Low cost, low carbon, electric heating system which rivals natural gas systems in running costs and over its lifetime will use decreasing amounts of carbon with no degradation in performance and no servicing costs.

Dimplex Quantum storage heaters are 27% cheaper to run than standard storage heaters and 47% cheaper than other electric heating systems, such as convector heaters panel heaters or electric radiators when used in conjunction with an Economy 7 electricity tariff.

Dimplex Quantum heaters boast an array of innovative energy saving features including fully customisable heating controls allowing up to four time and temperature settings for every day of the week. EcoDesign Lot 20 (recent electric heating efficiency regulations) have added adaptive start up and open window sensing technology, further boosting efficiency. By restricitng heat ouput when rapid temperature drop is deteted a a result of open windows or doors valuable heat and energy is saved, normal heat output resumes as soon as the doors or windows are closed.

SAP accredited and BEAB approved, Quantum and designed with safety and reliability in mind. They also come with a free 10 year guarantee (after online registration, terms and conditions apply).
